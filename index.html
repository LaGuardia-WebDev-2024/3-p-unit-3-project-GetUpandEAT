1. Preface

I really didn't know what to make this website about. I don't cook. I've never read a recipe in my life. My parents won't even let me touch the stove. Normally I'd make a website about something interesting that's going on in my life but there's nothing! My friends are always busy, my back hurts for some reason (not enough to make a website about it though), and I never have time to do the things I enjoy. So here's a website about a little bit of nothing (Whoa! That's the title!)

3. My TBR List

"Sometime soon" could mean right after you finish your current book, or it could mean months in the future when you're finally in the mood. I'm often the latter.
